INSPIRON 16.0

Since its inception in 2008, Inspiron has been hosted by The Placement and Training Centre,UVCE. With INSPIRON, UVCE provides its students an opportunity to host as well as showcase their skills by organizing events that focus on all the key areas for personal development of a student. This initiative from Placement and Training Centre gives every student a platform to compete with the best minds not only within this college, but also with those from different colleges across the state. In addition to testing the knowledge and awareness of students, the events are designed to help them improve their communication and marketing skills as well. This is where INSPIRON stands apart; it influences every student to not just being an Engineer but also to be an entrepreneur. This inter-collegiate techno-managerial extravaganza from UVCE has been received very well and has attracted a lot of crowd over the past years.
